Equipment Fitting
Total Hockey is proud to help you start your child’s hockey career. To make their experience safe & enjoyable, it is important to have equipment that fits correctly. There are 600 kids participating in the MSP area, so we are using a reservation system to reduce wait times and provide more personalized service. Let’s get started!

What to expect
  • A fitting takes about 30 minutes
  • No walk-ups will be accepted
  • Reservations are taken on a first come, first served basis
  • If you have more than 1 player in the program, a separate reservation for each child needs to be made
  • Items included are: Helmet, Shoulder pads, Gloves, Elbow pads, Breezers, Shin guards, Ice skates, Hockey socks, Hockey stick, Little Wild jersey, Equipment bag
  • During your fitting, your skates will also be sharpened at no charge
  • Additional items to consider purchasing – cloth & plastic tape, mouthguard, skate soakers & a skate towel (Do we make this a bundle at a special price???)
HOW TO SCHEDULE A FITTING APPOINTMENT
  • Click on the link below
  • Select “Equipment Fitting”
  • Select “Book”
  • Pick your preferred store location & click on “See Slots”
  • Scroll to the week containing your preferred date
  • Choose a day & time
  • If your preferred time isn’t displayed, click on the “Store Associate” button at the top right and switch to the Store Associate 2 to see if their schedule is open at the time you desire
  • If neither Associate is free for your desired time, you will need to select a different day, time or location
  • Once you select a time, enter the name & email used to register your Little Wild participant
  • Review & complete your booking
  • You will receive a confirmation email immediately, as well as reminder notifications 3 days & 1 day prior to your appointment
  • That email also contains links to change or cancel your appointment, as well as add the appointment to your electronic calendar
